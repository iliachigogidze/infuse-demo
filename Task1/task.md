## Role

**Project Manager / Product Manager / Business Analyst** on the Direct Phone Outreach (DPO) Platform.

## Project Overview

The DPO Platform aims to enhance operational efficiency across departments (DPO, CC, internal & external call-centers) by minimizing manual work, reducing human errors, and improving information accuracy.

## What You Need to Do

1. **Absorb the materials** — Watch the introduction video and review all provided documents (see Document Register / Readme). All resources are in the *Bootcamp - external* folder.

2. **Understand and extract essentials** — Comprehend the business process, stakeholder insights, and technical context. Collect and organize requirements from various sources.

3. **Think end-to-end** — Consider all stages of development and implementation: requirements gathering, analysis, development (back & front), testing, deployment, user training, and user documentation.

4. **Communicate effectively** — Ask questions, gather information from stakeholders, and clearly convey requirements including seemingly minor details.

5. **Propose solutions** — Use problem-solving skills to suggest viable, feasible, and practical solutions. All suggestions must be clarified and approved with customers/technical experts.
